Kitkat Lava French Toast

home

Description

KitKat lava French toast is a decadent dessert sandwich made by stuffing brioche bread with KitKat bars, dipping it in egg custard, frying until golden, and topping with molten chocolate cream.

Ingredients

  • 2–4 thick slices of white or brioche bread.
  • 1–2 packs of Nestlé KitKat Chocolate Bars
  • 25g whipping cream
  • 25g milk
  • Egg: 1 large
  • Cocoa Powder: 1 tsp
  • Sugar: 1 tbsp
  • Vanilla Extract: ½ tsp
  • Butter: 1–2 tbsp
  • Heavy Cream: 3 tbsp

Steps

  1. Prep the Bread: Take your thick slices of bread. Use a spoon or your thumb to press down the center of the bread firmly, creating a deep square "well" or indentation. Leave the edges raised to act as a wall for the lava.
  2. Make the Custard Soak: In a shallow bowl, whisk together the egg, milk, sugar, cocoa powder, and vanilla.
  3. Soak and Fry: Dip the bottom and the sides of the bread into the custard. Do not soak the inside of the "well" too much, or it will get soggy and collapse. Melt butter in a pan over medium-low heat. Fry the bread until the bottom and sides are golden and toasted.
  4. Create the Lava: While the toast is frying, place the crushed KitKat bars, heavy cream, and condensed milk in a microwave-safe bowl. Heat in 20-second intervals, stirring in between, until you have a smooth, thick, pourable chocolate ganache with bits of wafer.
  5. Assemble the "Lava": Place the toasted bread on a plate. Pour the warm KitKat mixture into the center well until it's overflowing slightly.
  6. Final Touches: Dust the edges with extra cocoa powder and top with more crushed KitKats for that signature crunch. Serve immediately while the center is still molte
